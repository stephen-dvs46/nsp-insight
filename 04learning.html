Introduction to Learning @ NSP

Northside Partnership is transforming the way learning is imparted to staff, making learning simpler, easier, and more engaging. The online mode of learning means our busy staff can learn at their own comfort and requirements. At Learning@NSP the content can be accessed, consumed, discussed, and shared at the time that suits you. Not everyone learns at the same pace now you will have the ability to define your own speed of learning instead of following the speed of a whole group. This will not do away with all classroom training or the need to read documents however it a big step in the right direction.

Learning@NSP will help Northside Partnership create and communicate new training content, policies, concepts, and ideas as well as "just in time" training" when changes are made to our computer systems (e.g. when Pobal make changes to IRIS).
